Tool description:

  • This tool can check the status of friendship links in batches to see if the friend chains added by the webmaster still have backlinks to their own website, so it is easy for the webmaster to delete and adjust invalid friend chains.
  • First fill in the following information (usually your own website address) into the form below, then fill in the links of the friend chain in batches, click "Start Detection" and wait for the results to return
  • Note: Friendship links may not be placed on the homepage of Youzhan, so be sure to fill in the link of the page where Youzhan places the YouChain, otherwise it will be judged that the YouChain has not been added.
Note: The detected information is generally the address provided to a friend site by yourself, that is, the address of your website
Note: Friends of Youzhan may not all be placed on the homepage, and wherever you put them, you will be provided with a link
